    ABOUT US

    "GVM Test House And Research Center", an ISO 9001-2015 certified, came into existence in 2019 with the aim of providing accurate and precise testing facilities to the manufacturing industries worldwide. Based in Navi-Mumbai, we at GVM are working consistently to ensure that your products meet all quality standards for acceptability in any market around the world.

    GVM Test House And Research Centre, is an NABL accredited and ISO Certified Testing service provider with the capability to provide best in class facilities to ascertain the quality and durability of building materials.

    We combine best-in-class technology with the extensive testing experience of our team to provide our clients accurate and factual representation of the conformity of material tests conducted.

    Our clientele include prominent builders, developers, material suppliers, manufacturers primarily providing their services for the Civil Engineering domain.

    No matter what the testing requirement is, our team at GVM Test House and Research Centre are always prepared to give costeffective, transparent and meaningful solutions to our clients
